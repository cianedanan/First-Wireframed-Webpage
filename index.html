THE WIKIPEDIA GAME

A free instruction guide

How to Play

The objective of this game is to get from the starting page to the goal page. Hit the blue play now button and your start page will open. From there you must click on the hyperlinks you think will get you closer to your goal page. The lower amount of clicks and time the more points you earn.

Daily Leaderboard

This leaderboard shows who has the most points today.

Latest Winner

You can see who did the last challenge the quickest. On display is how many times they've reached the endpage, which hyperlinks they clicked get there, how many points they earned for each win, how many clicks it took, and the time it took.
